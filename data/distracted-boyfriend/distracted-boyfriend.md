## Overview

Distracted Boyfriend is an object labeling stock photo series that became a popular internet meme. The photo shows a man turning his head to look at a woman walking by while his girlfriend looks on disapprovingly.

## Origin

The source image was taken by photographer Antonio Guillem and posted on iStock on November 1, 2015. The earliest known captioned version appeared in a Turkish Facebook group in early 2017.

## Spread

The meme gained widespread popularity in early 2017 after being shared on Instagram and Twitter. It has been used in numerous variations, including political parodies, brand comparisons, and recreations in real life and at events like San Diego Comic-Con.

## Cultural Impact

The meme was named "Meme of the Year" at the 10th annual Shorty Awards in 2018. It has been referenced in mainstream media, including the New York Times, and has inspired numerous derivatives and spin-offs.

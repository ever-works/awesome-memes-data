## Overview

Doge is an internet meme featuring Shiba Inu dogs, particularly Kabosu, accompanied by multiline text in Comic Sans font representing an internal monologue.

## Origin

The meme traces back to photos of Kabosu posted by Atsuko Sato on her blog in February 2010. The specific image of Kabosu sitting on a couch with a sideways glance became the iconic Doge image.

## Spread

The meme spread through Tumblr, Reddit, and 4chan in 2012-2013, leading to the creation of subreddits like /r/Doge and numerous single-topic blogs. It gained further popularity with variants such as Ironic Doge, Cheems, and Swole Doge.

## Variants

- Ironic Doge: Surreal or dark humor edits of the Doge image.
- Cheems: A Doge variant known for misspelling words with extra 'M's.
- Swole Doge: A muscular edit of the Doge character.
- Wew Lad: Often accompanied by altered Doge images or the 'Wrinkle Face' illustration.

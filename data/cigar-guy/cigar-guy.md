# Cigar Guy

## Overview
Cigar Guy refers to Rupesh Shingadia, a golf fan photographed at the 2010 Ryder Cup wearing a ginger wig, Groucho Marx moustache, and smoking a cigar. The image became an internet meme due to his distinctive appearance.

## Origin
The photo was taken by Daily Mail photographer Mark Pain during Tiger Woods' chip shot at the 18th green. The man's face was noticed for its enthusiastic expression and costume.

## Spread and Usage
After the photo went viral, Shingadia was identified as a 30-year-old investment analyst from south London. Internet users began photoshopping his face into other famous sporting events and scenes, creating a meme that highlights his positive, wide-eyed expression.

## Cultural Impact
The meme exemplifies how a candid photograph of a spectator can achieve fame through online sharing and photoshop remixes, becoming a symbol of enthusiastic fandom.

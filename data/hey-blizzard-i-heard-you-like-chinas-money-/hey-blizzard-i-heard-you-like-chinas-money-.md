# Hey Blizzard, I heard you like China's money ?

## Overview
The meme uses a still from the South Park episode 'Band in China', which mocks Hollywood and gaming companies for self-censoring to access the Chinese market. The caption directly addresses Blizzard following their controversy with player Blitz Chung.

## Origin
The image originates from South Park Studios, aired in 2019. The meme gained traction on Reddit after Blizzard's actions in October 2019.

## Popularity
This post received over 201,000 upvotes and thousands of awards, reflecting strong community sentiment.

## Usage
Used to criticize companies perceived as prioritizing market access over ethical stances, especially regarding human rights and censorship.

# Saugeen Stripper

## Overview
The Saugeen Stripper meme comes from a 2005 photograph of an 18-year-old woman performing a striptease at a dorm party at Saugeen-Maitland Hall, University of Western Ontario.

## Origin
After being asked to perform, the student did a striptease while attendees took photos. The images were uploaded to the Internet and quickly spread.

## Spread and Media Coverage
The incident was covered by Canadian media outlets such as the Toronto Star, The London Free Press, National Post, and local London A-Channel News, as well as outlets in the US, UK, and elsewhere.

## Cultural Impact
The meme sparked debate about how much control institutions have over student behavior on campus and contributed to the university's reputation as a party school.

# Imgflip Meme Templates

## Overview
Imgflip is a popular online platform for creating and sharing memes. Its meme templates page offers a searchable collection of blank meme images that serve as the foundation for meme creation.

## Features
- Over 1 million meme templates, constantly updated
- Search and browse by meme name, keyword, or popularity
- Easy-to-use meme generator: select a template, add top and bottom text, customize font and color
- Ability to upload your own images as templates
- Option to create GIF memes and video memes
- Meme storage and sharing via Imgflip account
- Community features: meme comments, ratings, and trending memes

## Pricing
- Free access to meme templates and basic meme creation with ads
- Imgflip Pro subscription (monthly or yearly) removes ads, unlocks advanced features like higher resolution exports, and provides extra storage

## Usage
Visit https://imgflip.com/memetemplates to browse templates. Click any template to open the meme generator, add your caption, and create your meme instantly.

## Notes
Imgflip also hosts a meme database where users can view popular memes and their origins, making it a resource for meme culture research.

# Madame Web

## Overview

Madame Web is a 2024 Marvel superhero film starring Dakota Johnson as the titular clairvoyant paramedic. The movie was widely criticized for its poor writing, acting, and plot, becoming a prime target for internet mockery.

## Origin

The meme originated from the film's trailer, released in late 2023. A particular line of dialogue—"He was in the Amazon with my mom when she was researching spiders right before she died"—was noted for its awkward exposition and unnatural delivery. This line quickly spread as a copypasta across platforms like Twitter, Reddit, and TikTok, often being repeated verbatim or used in ironic contexts.

## Why It's Funny

The humor stems from the line's clumsy writing and serious delivery, creating a stark contrast that invites ridicule. The meme exemplifies the internet's tendency to embrace and amplify perceived failures in media, turning a marketing misstep into a shared joke.

## Spread

Although the line was removed from the final cut of the film, the meme persisted. The film's press tour, marked by Dakota Johnson's eccentric interviews and an on-air earthquake interruption, further cemented its status as a meme goldmine. Audiences flocked to theaters not for the film itself but to participate in the ongoing joke, cementing Madame Web's place in meme history as a beloved "so bad it's good" phenomenon.

## Legacy

Madame Web joins a long tradition of films that gain notoriety through internet memes, demonstrating how online communities can reframe critical failure into collective enjoyment and participation.

# Parked Domain Girl

## Overview
The Parked Domain Girl is a stock photograph of a young blonde college woman that became widely circulated online as the default image for parked domain pages operated by Demand Media.

## Origin
The photo was uploaded to iStockphoto in 2005 by Dustin Steller. After an online campaign to identify the woman, it was revealed she is the sister of Dustin Steller. The picture was taken in Unity Village, Kansas City area.

## Spread and Usage
Because Demand Media buys generic domain names and fills them with advertising, the image appears on countless parked domains. Internet users have created hundreds of variations, adding captions, altering the background, or inserting the girl into other scenes. A notable example is the series of oil paintings commissioned from orderartwork.com by Parker Ito.

## Cultural Impact
The meme illustrates how a simple stock image can achieve widespread recognition through repetitive exposure on parked domains, highlighting the economics of domain parking and ad-driven content.

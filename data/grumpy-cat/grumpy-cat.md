# Grumpy Cat

## Overview

Grumpy Cat, born Tardar Sauce (April 4, 2012 – May 14, 2019), was an American internet celebrity cat known for her permanently "grumpy" facial appearance, which made her a popular subject for memes and online humor.

## Origin

The meme began when a photograph of Tardar Sauce was posted on September 22, 2012, on the social news website Reddit by her owner's brother, Bryan Bundesen. Reddit users quickly created image macros and parodies, depicting her with cynical, humorous captions.

## Meaning and Humor

The humor stems from the cat's perpetually dissatisfied expression, which is juxtaposed with captions expressing negative or sarcastic viewpoints on everyday situations. The contrast between the cute animal and the grumpy demeanor creates an ironic, relatable comedy.

## Popularity

As of June 3, 2023, Grumpy Cat had 8.3 million total likes on Facebook, 2.5 million followers on Instagram, 1.3 million followers on Twitter, and 275,000 subscribers on YouTube. She won numerous awards, including the Webby Award for Meme of the Year in 2013 and a Lifetime Achievement Award at the 2013 Friskies.

## Merchandise and Media

Grumpy Cat's likeness was licensed for a wide range of products, including T-shirts, mugs, stuffed toys, calendars, books, comic books, and a video game titled *Grumpy Cat: Unimpressed*. She also starred in the Lifetime film *Grumpy Cat's Worst Christmas Ever* (2014) and appeared in the documentary *Lil Bub & Friendz*.

## Legacy

Following her death in May 2019 due to complications from a urinary tract infection, Grumpy Cat's influence endures as one of the most iconic internet memes. Her image continues to be used in memes, and she remains a reference point for "grumpy" humor online.

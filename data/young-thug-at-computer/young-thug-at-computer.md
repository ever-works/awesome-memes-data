## Overview

Young Thug at computer refers to a viral photo of rappers Young Thug and Lil Durk working in a studio, fixated on a computer screen. The ambiguous nature of their concentration led to widespread meme speculation.

## Origin

The photograph was taken in 2018 during a studio session where Young Thug and Lil Durk were collaborating on music. The image shows both artists leaning towards a computer monitor, appearing deeply focused.

## Meme Usage

Internet users began creating humorous captions imagining what the rappers were looking at, such as:
- Planning a bank heist
- Playing classic games like Minesweeper or Solitaire
- Watching unusual videos
- Reading contracts or lyrics

The meme format typically places text above and below the image, describing the imagined scenario.

## Cultural Impact

While not as enduring as some memes, it exemplifies how candid celebrity photos can be repurposed for absurdist humor online, particularly within hip-hop fan communities.

# Dog Poop Girl

## Overview
The Dog Poop Girl meme originates from a 2005 photograph taken in the Seoul Metropolitan Subway showing a woman who ignored requests to clean up after her dog's feces.

## Origin
Passenger Miss Kim captured photos of the incident and posted them on a popular Korean website. The images quickly became one of the most searched terms on Korean web portals.

## Spread and Vigilantism
Internet users launched a vigilante campaign to identify the woman, publishing her personal information online. She was linked to a university, which received massive internet traffic. The humiliation reportedly led her to quit school.

## Cultural Impact
The meme illustrates the power of online crowdsourcing and the consequences of public shaming in the digital age, sparking debates about privacy and internet vigilantism.

## Overview

The expanding brain meme is an image macro series that shows a sequence of brain images growing in size, each accompanied by text that represents increasing levels of intelligence or enlightenment.

## Origin

First appearing in 2017 on platforms like Tumblr and 4chan, the meme began with users bragging about their brain sizes, pairing photos of different-sized brains with "smart" sounding words.

## Meaning and Usage

Typically used to depict a progression of ideas, from mundane to highly intellectual, often with a sarcastic or humorous twist. The format can be adapted to show any escalating scale, such as "who-whom-whomst" progression of words.

## Variations

Many variations exist, substituting the brain images with other expanding objects (e.g., galaxies, profit charts) while keeping the same textual escalation format.

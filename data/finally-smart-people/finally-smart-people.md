# Finally some smart people

## Overview

The 'Finally some smart people' meme is an image macro that pairs a photograph with the caption 'Finally, some smart people' to convey sarcastic approval.

## Meaning and Origin

The image typically shows a man in a suit looking pleased or surprised. The phrase is used ironically to praise someone for displaying basic intelligence or common sense, implying that such behavior is rare or unexpected.

## Usage

This meme is commonly used in comment sections, forums, and social media to react to posts where someone demonstrates obvious logic or knowledge, often highlighting the low expectations of the group.

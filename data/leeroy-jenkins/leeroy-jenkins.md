## Overview

Leeroy Jenkins refers to a viral video uploaded in 2005 showing a player in World of Warcraft ignoring group strategy and charging into the Upper Blackrock Spire dungeon while shouting his name. The resulting wipe became a widely shared meme.

## Origin

The video was created by World of Warcraft player Ben Schulz, whose character Leeroy Jenkins performed the infamous charge on May 11, 2005. The clip spread via forums and early video-sharing sites.

## Usage

The phrase \"Leeroy Jenkins!\" is used to call out premature or ill-considered actions, often in gaming or workplace contexts. It has been referenced in other media, merchandise, and even official game content.

## Cultural Impact

The meme remains recognizable over a decade later, illustrating the staying power of early internet culture.

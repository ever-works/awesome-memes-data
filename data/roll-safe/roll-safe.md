# Roll Safe

Roll Safe is an Internet meme featuring a still image of British filmmaker and actor Kayode Ewumi portraying the character Reece Simpson (also known as Roll Safe) tapping his finger on his head.

## Origin

The character Roll Safe first appeared in Vine in 2015. The Hood Documentary episode was published in June 2016. The image became a reaction image on British Twitter accounts later in 2016, and its popularity surged in late January 2017, particularly within the Black Twitter community.

## Usage

The meme is typically used with captions that mock poor decision-making and failures in critical thinking. A common format is "You can't X, if there is no Y". For example, "You can't fail an exam if there is no exam".

## Cultural Impact

Roll Safe was featured on numerous 'best memes of 2017' lists from outlets such as Thrillist, Complex, BuzzFeed News, The Daily Dot, Insider, BBC News, PCMag, Washington Post, PopBuzz, and MTV UK. It was also a finalist in the 10th Shorty Awards. In 2024, The Daily Dot referred to it as "one of the most indelible images" of the 2010s Internet.

## Overview

The "Eminem ain't fat" meme uses a picture of the rapper Eminem, often accompanied by the caption asserting that he is not fat. It responds to recurring online commentary about his weight.

## Meaning

The meme is used to push back against body‑shaming remarks, promoting a light‑hearted defense of Eminem’s appearance. It also exemplifies how meme culture repeatedly jokes about celebrity physiques.

## Origin

The exact origin is unclear, but the phrase gained traction on social media platforms where users discuss Eminem’s looks, particularly during periods when his weight changed noticeably.

## Usage

Typically shared as an image macro on Reddit, Twitter, and other platforms to humorously dismiss criticisms of Eminem’s body.

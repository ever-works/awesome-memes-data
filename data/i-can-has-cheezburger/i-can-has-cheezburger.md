# I Can Has Cheezburger?

## Overview
The 'I Can Has Cheezburger?' meme is an image macro that pairs a picture of a cat with a caption written in a playful, broken English dialect known as lolspeak.

## Origin
The meme originated from a photograph of a cat posted on somethingawful.com in 2007. The image was paired with the caption 'I Can Has Cheezburger?' and shared on the site's 'Image Macros' forum. The phrase quickly spread, leading to the creation of the ICanHasCheezburger.com website in January 2007 by Eric Nakagawa (known as 'Happycat') and Kari Unebasami (known as 'Professeur Burger').

## Spread
The site popularized the lolspeak language and the image macro format, inspiring countless derivative memes and contributing to the rise of user-generated humor sites. The meme's success demonstrated the power of simple, repeatable formats in viral content.

## Cultural Impact
'I Can Has Cheezburger?' is credited with helping to establish the image macro as a dominant meme format and influencing the development of internet culture around animal pictures and humorous captions. It also led to the publication of books and a line of merchandise.

## Notes
The meme is often referenced in discussions about the early evolution of internet memes and the role of user communities in meme propagation.
